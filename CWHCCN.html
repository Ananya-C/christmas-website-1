How We Celebrate Christmas Now

The celebration starts from Christmas Eve i.e. 24th December every year, as per the Gregorian calendar. The next day is Christmas Day (the main day), which is celebrated on 25th December. The 3rd day is known as Boxing Day, which is celebrated one day after Christmas i.e. on 26th December.
Christmas is celebrated in different forms across more than 160 nations and among billions of people. Traditionally, Christmas decorations start with - decorations of the house with stars, colorful lights, a Christmas tree, and the Crib.
Christmas celebration is then followed by preparation of cakes and cookies. People also sing Christmas carols (songs) expressing their joy and happiness, welcoming the Jesus Christ.
